Terms of Service

Payment

Commissions require full pay before I start working on them.

Prices listed only serve as a guideline for how much you might pay for, more complex requests in your commission will cost more. I will tell you the price once you pitch your idea.

I only accept payment on Paypal.

Commission Process

I will contact you as soon as I start drawing your commission

I will send over progress of the art as it's made

Major changes to posing after the sketch phase will require additional pay, as I would have to cancel a near completed drawing and start again.

Refunding

Refunds are only eligible before the first work in progress update is sent.

I can cancel a commission for any reason before and during the process, If this happens you will be fully refunded.

Refusal


I will not draw any of the following:

  • NSFW/Porn
  • Humans
  • Hateful content
  • Anything I might be uncomfortable drawing.
